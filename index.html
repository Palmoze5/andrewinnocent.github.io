About Me

I’m a motivated and resourceful full-stack web developer with a passion for solving problems and helping people succeed. I have 10 years of experience in working effectively in teams and engaging with stakeholders. With the web development skills recently learned at GA and the wide set of skills from my previous work experience, I'm excited to use them at a mission-minded organization.


I always had an interest in technology and how it works. While assisting the outreach efforts of a couple non-profits by setting up their websites (thank you, Squarespace!), I got to dabble a little into the world of coding. I asked myself, “Is there a way for me to go deeper and possibly work as a web developer?” That led me to General Assembly and the Web Developer Immersive bootcamp! This was the fastest way for me to change careers and enter the exciting web development world.
